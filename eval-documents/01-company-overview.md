# Acme Corp — Company Overview

## About Us

Acme Corp was founded in 2019 by CEO **Maya Patel** and CTO **Jordan Liu** in Austin, Texas. The company builds enterprise project-management software used by over 3,200 organizations worldwide.

## Mission

Our mission is to eliminate wasted time in project coordination by providing an intelligent, unified workspace.

## Core Values

1. **Transparency** — All decisions are documented and shared openly.
2. **Customer Obsession** — Every feature ships only after user research validates it.
3. **Ship Fast, Learn Faster** — Two-week sprint cycles with continuous deployment.
4. **Inclusive by Default** — Our product meets WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.
