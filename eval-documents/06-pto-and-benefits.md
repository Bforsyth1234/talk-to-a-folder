# PTO & Benefits Policy

**Effective:** January 1, 2025  
**Owner:** Layla Kim, VP of People & Culture

## Paid Time Off

- **Vacation**: 20 days per year for all full-time employees (accrues monthly).
- **Sick leave**: 10 days per year (no carryover).
- **Personal days**: 3 days per year.
- **Parental leave**: 16 weeks fully paid for all new parents (birth, adoption, or foster).
- **Bereavement**: 5 days for immediate family, 3 days for extended family.

Unused vacation days carry over up to a maximum of **5 days** into the next calendar year.

## Holidays

Acme Corp observes **11 company holidays** per year:

1. New Year's Day
2. MLK Jr. Day
3. Presidents' Day
4. Memorial Day
5. Juneteenth
6. Independence Day
7. Labor Day
8. Indigenous Peoples' Day
9. Thanksgiving Day
10. Day after Thanksgiving
11. Christmas Day

Additionally, employees receive **2 floating holidays** to use at their discretion.

## Health Benefits

- **Medical**: Choice of PPO or HDHP plan; Acme covers 90% of employee premiums and 70% of dependent premiums.
- **Dental**: 100% preventive coverage; Acme covers 80% of premiums.
- **Vision**: Covered in full for employees; $150 annual frame allowance.
- **Mental Health**: Free access to the Calm app and 12 therapy sessions per year through Lyra Health.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

Acme matches **100% of employee contributions up to 4%** of base salary. Vesting is immediate.

## Other Perks

- $1,500/year **learning & development** stipend (books, courses, conferences)
- $500/year **home office** stipend
- Monthly **wellness stipend** of $75 (gym, fitness classes, etc.)
- Annual company offsite (all expenses paid) held every **October**
